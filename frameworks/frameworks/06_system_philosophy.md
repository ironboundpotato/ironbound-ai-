Drift occurs because:
- meaning accumulates entropy  
- context diffuses  
- linguistic approximation compounds  
- assumptions fill gaps  
- generative processes prefer expansion  
- multi-step tasks destabilize intent  

Drift is gravity.

IRONBOUND-AI is the structure that resists that gravity.
